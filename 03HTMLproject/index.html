Get to know me

Hi! My name is Halli. I grew up in a small town called Star Valley, Wyoming. I am currently going to school at Utah Vally University studying Art & Design, with an emphasis in Graphic Design.

My goal

Growing up, my parents were both really into health and fitness. My dad owned and coached at a little CrossFit gym, and he influenced and helped me to grow to love health and fitness as well. Moving out of the house and living on my own made it harder for me to stay disciplined. It was honestly easier and cheaper to eat Raman Noodles. Eating healthy can get so expensive, and it honestly can be tough to do that while being a college student. For a while, I’ve been learning more and more how to food prep and eat better, while not spending too much money! So, I have decided to share a few simple recipes that I’ve created, and my goal is to improve my diet and create a healthy lifestyle, and to inspire those who are in the same boat.

Benefits of eating healthier

  • More energy

  • Clearer mind
  • Can help you acheive your physical goals
